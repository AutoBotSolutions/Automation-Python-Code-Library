### update_log

```python
def update_log(self, message):
        timestamp = time.strftime("%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
        log_entry = f"{timestamp} - {message}\n"
        self.running_log += log_entry
        self.log_area.insert(tk.END, log_entry)
        self.log_area.see(tk.END)
```
